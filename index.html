About Frontier Labs

Frontier Labs is a clean, modern prototyping and design studio. We specialize in 3D printing, product design, laser cutting, and R&D for entrepreneurs, engineers, and teams with bold ideas. Whether you're creating a one-off invention or scaling a business, we turn ideas into reality with a practical, hands-on approach.
