About Frontier Labs

Frontier Labs is a North Idaho-based prototyping and design studio blending engineering discipline with extreme creativity. We help you bring your ideas to life—whether you're a startup, entrepreneur, or a business looking to stand out.

Our workflow is hands-on and agile: from CAD to 3D printing, laser cutting, sustainable materials, and research collaborations. You dream it, we design and build it.
Advanced Prototyping
Rapid 3D printing, functional models, and iterative testing for fast feedback and breakthrough innovation.
Mechanical Design
High-precision CAD, parametric modeling, and product design using Fusion 360, SolidWorks, and more.
Laser Cutting & Custom Fabrication
Bespoke parts, signage, templates, and more—delivered with speed and accuracy for real-world use.
R&D Partnerships
Collaborate on research, product development, and materials engineering for your next breakthrough.
Extreme Sports Engineering
Design and prototype gear, tools, and accessories that push the boundaries of performance.
